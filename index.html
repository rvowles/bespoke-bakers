What we do

We specialise in organic sourdough, including flours from a Demeter certified grower in the South Island. We also do a variety of other enriched doughs from time to time including Shokupan, Çörek and Simit.

Our bread is aged for 24 hours before baking to develop flavour, but if you are need of a large amount of pizza dough for an event, we can do that for you as well.

Our most popular sourdoughs are chilli olives with whole wheat, black olives and black sesame with whole rye, and pumpkin & sunflower whole rye.

Bakery Times

We are located in Glendowie, Auckland, New Zealand. We generally cater to pick-up and local delivery by bicycle, so if you within a few kms, we can supply you!

We bake (almost) every Sunday, ready for midday. We can however bake outside of these times on special request.

How to order

Please contact us on our Instagram via DM or Chief Baker. We email out an update each Wednesday which is a form you can fill in what you would like that week (if any). Our cut-off time is Friday for Sourdough and Saturday mid-day for yeast based loaves.

Other services

We offer a range of other bread related services on request including fresh flaking of barley and oats (for porridge, museli or granola) and whole grain milling (rye, wheat and barley, in small amounts).
